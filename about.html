About Me

I am a current Masters in Artificial Intelligence student at The University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA). I received my Bachelor's in Electrical and Computer Engineering in the Fall of 2021 from UTSA. In the Summer of 2021, I was a participant in the National Science Foundation, Research Experience for Undergraduates at UTSA, this program is what started my journey into Artificial Intelligence and my interest in Reinforcement Learning. I learned about various Machine Learning topics and worked on a project for Human Guided Reinforcement Learning. Throughout my Undergraduate degree, I had the honor of receiving the Outstanding Undergraduate Award in Fall of 2021. In the Fall of 2021 I also worked on an Electrical Engineering team called SDQ Engineering to create a Low Volume Chicken Seasoning Machine Sponsored by HEB this project was a collaboration with a Mechanical Engineering Team called Mechanical Velox. This project was presented at UTSA's Tech Symposium and came first place in the competition. After graduating I took a semester to learn more about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Reinforcement Learning. I had the opportunity to help teach Undergraduate and Graduate students who were new to Machine Learning about what Machine Learning is and how to write Machine Learning code. I also created learning materials for students in my lab to learn Python, Machine Learning, Neural Networks, and Reinforcement Learning, these provided links as well as explanations to create a foundation in the topics. In the Fall of 2022, I joined the the Masters in Artificial Intelligence at UTSA as a part of the first cohort. In my masters I currently am performing research in Human Guided Reinforcement Learning as well as in Aerospace Autonomous Controls. If you would like to learn more or ask any questions, please email me at devin.white@my.utsa.edu
